About

A Lifelong Voice
in Rhythm

Marcus Webb is a Vancouver-based jazz drummer and bandleader with a deeply rooted love for the art of groove. Drawing from the tradition of hard bop and post-bop while weaving in contemporary influences, his playing is at once conversational and commanding.

Whether leading his quartet through a late-night club set or sitting in as a first-call session drummer, Marcus brings a rare combination of technical precision and musical intuition that makes every performance feel both spontaneous and inevitable.

He is available to book as a solo musician, trio, quartet, or full jazz ensemble for live venues, corporate events, private celebrations, and recording sessions.

About

A Voice in Rhythm

Mason Weaver is a Vancouver-based drummer equally at home behind a loud rock kit or brushing jazz standards late into the night. His playing draws from both worlds — raw energy and nuanced feel — and that combination makes every performance feel alive.

A first-call drummer for live gigs, studio sessions, and collaborative projects, Mason brings technical precision and genuine musicality to whatever the room calls for. His experience spans original rock bands, jazz ensembles, and session work across genres.

Available for live venues, corporate events, private celebrations, and recording sessions — solo through full band configurations.
